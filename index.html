My latest projects

Stock Market Prediction using Artificial Neural Networks

The SMP model we have worked around represents the existing data in the form of a main dataset, closing price and opening price. The targeted companies are the big 4 comprising of Amazon, Google, Microsoft and Apple. The output comprises of Sales Volume, Daily Returns, also a 4x4 comparison between the companies. The mapping of correlation between the closing price is done and also the risk factor is calculated. The data model works after we train the data and model it. The model then predicts and helps the visualization of the data. On a scale of 5 the accuracy of the model is 4.7 which is based on several constituting factors.

Employee Payroll Management System

The project Employee Payroll Management System is a project which helps in managing the payroll record of employees. With the help of the layout which allows them to enter the data of the employees and stores them in a table using a MySQL Database. The functions are carried out using the Insert, Delete, Update and Retrieve buttons. The Weekly Salary button when used carries out the calculation of Tax, Gross Pay, Net Pay and Overtime (if any) according to the algorithm programmed. The purpose of this project majorly is to help the groups of people who are employed on an hourly basis in the rural and semi urban areas.
